Why This Exists

Every year thousands of people celebrate Juhannus in Tampere. Most celebrate responsibly, but public spaces inevitably end up with litter, bottles and waste.

Instead of waiting for someone else to solve the problem, we're spending less than an hour helping the city we all enjoy.

No politics. No lectures. No complaining. Just action.
